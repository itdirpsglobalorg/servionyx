About Us

Art-Square Property Management provides professional services tailored for luxury residential complexes. Our mission is to safeguard your investment and create a worry-free living experience. We combine legal compliance, transparency, and premium service standards.

We are the dedicated property management company exclusively responsible for the Art-Square residential complex. With extensive knowledge of Cyprus’ legal framework for common property and years of experience in luxury residential management, we combine professionalism with personal attention.

Our Services

🛠️ Technical Services

  • Preventive and emergency repairs
  • Pest control, sanitation, and drainage checks
  • Utility coordination (water, electricity, waste services)

🌿 Common Area Management

  • Cleaning and landscaping services
  • Pool, gym, sauna & playground maintenance
  • Security systems and gated access

🗂️ Administration

  • Organization of Annual General Meetings (AGM)
  • Management Committee support and reporting
  • Budget planning and transparent accounting
  • Compliance with Cyprus Law for common properties
  • Insurance and safety checks

🏡 Rental & Tenant Services

  • Promotion and marketing of apartments for rent
  • Tenant screening and contracts
  • Rent collection and payment control
  • Full maintenance & tenant support

Living at Art-Square

Luxury lifestyle, professionally managed.

Art-Square is more than a residential complex – it’s a lifestyle. With premium facilities such as a swimming pool, gym, multifunctional meeting room, sauna, landscaped gardens, table tennis room, and children’s playground, our role is to ensure these amenities are maintained to the highest standards for all residents to enjoy.

Budget & Transparency

Management expenses are distributed fairly based on each apartment’s indoor floor area, in full compliance with Cyprus common property law.

For Art-Square, the budget is calculated at €2.50 per m² of indoor space.

Clear, transparent, and predictable.
